Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

🔒 Our Core Promise

MedNet Diagnostics AI never stores, shares, or sells patient data. All inputs are processed in real-time and discarded immediately after generating the HEART Score assessment.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ No patient names, MRNs, or identifiers
  • ❌ No protected health information (PHI) retention
  • ❌ No tracking pixels, analytics cookies, or third-party scripts
  • ❌ No data sold to advertisers, researchers, or third parties

What We DO Process (Temporarily)

When you use the MedNet AI demo, we temporarily process the following to generate your assessment:

  • ✅ Symptoms entered (e.g., "chest pressure", "sweating")
  • ✅ Risk factors selected (e.g., "smoking", "hypertension")
  • ✅ Troponin value and ECG findings
  • ✅ Age and demographic context (for guideline alignment)

⚠️ This data exists only in memory during the API call (<1 second) and is never written to disk, logged, or transmitted beyond the secure Cloud Run endpoint.

HIPAA-Aware Architecture

While MedNet AI is not a covered entity under HIPAA (we don't handle PHI), we design our system to exceed HIPAA standards:

  • 🔐 Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • 🔐 Zero data retention policy enforced at infrastructure level
  • 🔐 Audit logs for system access (no patient data)
  • 🔐 Regular security assessments and penetration testing

Enterprise & On-Premise Options

For hospital systems requiring maximum control:

  • 🏥 Deploy MedNet AI behind your firewall
  • 🏥 Integrate with your existing identity provider (SSO)
  • 🏥 Full audit trail and compliance reporting
  • 🏥 Dedicated instance with no shared infrastructure
